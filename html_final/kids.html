kids | il bambini


  • Kids Portions:
    All of our portions are availalble in 'kids' sizes for 30% off the menu price.

  • Special Kids Menu:
    Special kids menu are available upon request. The menu changes weekly.

  • Italian Tuesdays:
    Every Tuesday night, we hold a special 'learn Italian night' for children. Ages 10 and under, please.

  • Square Pizza Saturdays
    Every Saturday, children can order a small, thin-crust, square-style pizza for $12 during normal adult happy hour times

  • Free Dessert:
    All children under age 12 receive a free small semifreddo dessert. (Offer valid with the order of a kids portion meal only).

  • Mac and Cheese Mondays:
    We serve Homemade Mac and Cheese to childen under age 12 every Monday for $8.99. Bring your children in!

  • Bottomless Pasta
    Children under age 12 can order all you can eat pasta on Sundays for $10. Butter and Meat Sauce only.

  • Topolino Thursdays
    On the first Thursday of every month, Topolino Mouse (Micky Mouse) makes an apperance at Kent Ristorante at 5:30pm.
