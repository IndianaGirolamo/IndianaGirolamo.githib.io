Our History

Welcome to Kent Ristorante. We began as a small Italian take-out place in 1905 that soon became a world-renowned Boston landmark. We have been featured on the Food Network, Drive-ins and Dives &, and the Phantom Gourmet! Also, our restaurant has been featured in many newspapers, and magazines.


Today

Today, we have one of the oldest and state-of-the-art upscale Italian restaurants in Boston. We are very passionate about what we do and will always strive to make your experience memorable! Customers love our food, our friendly service, and our upscale atmosphere. Grazie!
