specials | il speciali


  • Student Sundays:
    Current students on Boston campuses receive 30% off their meal on Sundays only

  • Italian Holiday Discount:
    5% off your total bill during Italian-only observed holidays (example: Epiphany )

  • Group Dessert:
    Parties with 15 or more people receive a free small semifreddo as a dessert

  • Cannoli Fridays
    Every Friday, we serve Cannolis for $6.99

  • Raw Garlic for Mussolini's Death:
    On April 28th of every year, customers will receive a piece of raw garlic with every meal

  • Wine Wednesday:
    Your first glass of our house wine is 30% off every Wednesday

  • Unification Trivia:
    On March 17th of every year, we hold an Italian-themed trivia night. The winner receives a $50 gift card to Kent Ristorante

  • Shrimp Scampi Summers
    Kent Ristorante has Shrimp Scampi on the menu during the summer for $15
