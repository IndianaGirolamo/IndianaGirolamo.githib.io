A Little Bit About Us...

Although Accurate Graphics has been in business for about 20 years, owner Peppi Bolognese has been in the sign industry since its inception. Starting as an installer over 35 years ago, Peppi Bolognese has worked his way up to become the owner of his own company. 35 years of work has qualified him to become an expert in the business.

Our company consists of the right mix of people to serve you with whatever you need. We are here with you every step of the sign buying process: from estimation to completion. Let 35 years of sign installation work for you!
